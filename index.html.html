The visit in company HODEY

Introduction to company HODEY:

The firm Vital Centrum HODEY KG is a medical supply store and logistics center which is locating in Kamp Lintfort and in a few more cities in Germany.The company deals with rehabilitation,orthopedics - specialized for elderly and people with disabilities.It also provide orthopedic shoes, dentures,homecare and medical equipment.

For many years,HODEY has been working as a holistic provider of health service.In addition,the experience which the company maintain,stands for innovative customer soluions,including individual care with orthopedics in the areas of prevention,therapy,mobility and care.

The company continuously invest modernization,techni="cal equipment and qualifications of employees.It helps people to provide good care,relieve temporary or permanent disabilities,heal and re-mobilize them.In addition,the firm supply quality products from leading manufacturers of the industry and combine the range of medical services with meaningful health services and complementary products.Moreover,HODEY works at optimising of the services and the products,in order to re-innovate new technologies like they are already deal with 3D -Technic and using high quality textile materials for the products.It provide an electronic monitoring of the body and examine its condition.Our group of the Interdisciplinary project of the Fabrication of care was joining to this company,in which we was pleased to understand how the system is working and we was glad that the company has an a lot of ideas for making a project with them and to have a opportunity to work there.We took some pictures to documentate the process of working process of the machines and also some equipment.
