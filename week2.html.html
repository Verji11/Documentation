Introduction to company HODEY:

The firm Vital Centrum HODEY KG is a medical supply store and logistics center which is locating in Kamp Lintfort and in a few more cities in Germany.The company deals with rehabilitation,orthopedics - specialized for elderly and people with disabilities.It also provide orthopedic shoes, dentures,homecare and medical equipment.

For many years,HODEY has been working as a holistic provider of health service.In addition,the experience which the company maintain,stands for innovative customer soluions,including individual care with orthopedics in the areas of prevention,therapy,mobility and care.
